Our Story, Your Style

Discover the passion, innovation, and craftsmanship behind Smart Garment Factory

Redefining Fashion Manufacturing

Founded in 2018, Smart Garment Factory began with a simple vision: to revolutionize the fashion industry by combining cutting-edge technology with sustainable practices. What started as a small team of passionate designers and engineers has grown into a leading on-demand garment manufacturing platform.

Our journey has been driven by a commitment to innovation, quality, and environmental responsibility. We've pioneered AI-driven design tools, automated production processes, and a zero-waste manufacturing approach that sets new standards in the industry.

Today, we partner with brands, designers, and individual creators worldwide to bring their fashion visions to life with unprecedented speed, precision, and sustainability.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape everything we do

Our Mission

To democratize fashion manufacturing by making it accessible, sustainable, and efficient through technology. We empower creators at all levels to bring their designs to life without compromising quality or the environment.

Our Vision

To transform the global fashion industry into a circular, on-demand ecosystem where every garment is made to order, personalized to perfection, and produced with minimal environmental impact.

Our Promise

We commit to ethical manufacturing, sustainable materials, and innovative solutions that reduce waste while delivering exceptional quality and fit for every customer.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our evolution as an industry leader

June 2018

Company Founded

Smart Garment Factory was established with a small team of 5 people in a shared workspace, focusing on sustainable small-batch production.

March 2019

First AI Prototype

We developed our first AI-powered design assistant, reducing design-to-production time by 40% compared to traditional methods.

November 2020

Series A Funding

Secured $8M in funding to expand our automated production facilities and develop our proprietary inventory management system.

May 2021

Sustainability Certification

Achieved Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS) certification for our organic fabric line and zero-waste production process.

January 2023

International Expansion

Opened our first overseas production hub in Portugal, serving European markets with reduced carbon footprint from shipping.

Present

Industry Leadership

Now serving over 500 brands worldwide with a team of 120+ innovators, producing 50,000+ garments monthly with 75% less waste than traditional manufacturing.
